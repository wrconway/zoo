Zoos introduce people to animals. Each year, approximately 700,000 visitors from all 100 counties in North Carolina and all 50 States of the US travel to Asheboro, North Carolina, to visit with our animals. The Zoo is home to approximately 1,600 individual specimens representing more than 225 species.
